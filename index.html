Install & Usage

[Mac]
wget http://tnoda78.github.io/hozumi-command/tools/darwin/hozumi
chmod +x hozumi
./hozumi
[Linux amd64]

./hozumi

./hozumi
./hozumi ほずみ ながた みのる
./hozumi --speed high
./hozumi --speed high --cool
